Match Highlights: India vs Australia, 5th Test, Day 2

Morning Session: Early Breakthroughs

India resumed their innings on 35/2, hoping to stabilize the innings. However, Starc made an early impact, removing Rohit Sharma for 23 in the 7th over. Kohli, too, fell for 17 as Boland continued his impressive spell. By lunch, India was struggling at 75/4.

Afternoon Session: Reddy's Counterattack

In a surprising turn, Nitiesh Reddy launched a counterattack, smashing a quick 45 off 30 balls, including two sixes. Washington Sundar provided steady support with 22 runs, but both fell in quick succession, leaving India precariously placed at 125/6.

Evening Session: Lower-Order Resistance

Ravindra Jadeja and Rishabh Pant batted cautiously to extend India's lead. Pant hit two boundaries before being dismissed by Boland. India ended the day at 141/6, with a healthy lead of 145 runs, setting up an intriguing Day 3.

Key Moments of the Day

  • Starc dismissed Rohit Sharma early in the morning session.
  • Nitiesh Reddy's explosive 45 off 30 balls changed the momentum.
  • Scott Boland's tight spell of 3/40 kept Australia in contention.
